👋 Welcome to the GNHS Lesson Plan Portal

This portal is the official digital repository for lesson plans of Guinbirayan National High School, Guinbirayan. It is designed to help teachers organize, share, and access lesson plans efficiently across all grade levels and departments.

Lesson plans are organized by School Year and divided into Term 1, Term 2, and Term 3. Inside each term you will find the three departments — Junior High School (JHS), Science Technology & Engineering (STE), and Senior High School (SHS) — each with their respective grade level cards.

📌 How to use: Click Lesson Plans in the navigation bar, choose a School Year and Term at the top, then browse by Department and Grade Level. To upload, click the yellow Upload button at any time.
